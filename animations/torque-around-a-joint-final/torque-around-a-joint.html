Muscles, Joints, and Torque

A simplified anatomy lever model showing how load, moment arms, and muscle insertion distance change the force a muscle must produce.

Arm: elbow flexion

Elbow fulcrum, load at the hand, muscle effort near the elbow.

Model controls

Load force22 lb

Mechanical advantage0.11
Effort force × effort moment arm = load force × load moment arm

Because the muscle inserts close to the fulcrum, it must generate a much larger force than the load.
