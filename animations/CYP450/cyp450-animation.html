Step 1: Non-polar substrate approaches CYP450

Many CYP450 substrates are non-polar molecules, including many drugs, toxins, and hormones.

Another molecule can compete for the active site, reducing metabolism of the original substrate.

Changing the number or activity of CYP450 enzymes changes how quickly substrates are metabolized.
