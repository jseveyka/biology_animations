Negative Feedback Loop of Tanning in Skin

Stage 1 of 7

Baseline Skin

The skin cross-section begins with little melanin. Melanocytes sit near the basal epidermis and extend branches between keratinocytes.

Feedback loop

  1. UV exposure
  2. ↓
  3. Melanocyte activation
  4. ↓
  5. Melanin production
  6. ↓
  7. Darker epidermis
  8. ↓
  9. Less UV penetration
  10. ↓
  11. Reduced melanocyte stimulation
