Key Ideas

  1. The Moon's gravity creates two high-tide bulges: one facing the Moon and one on the opposite side of Earth.
  2. As Earth rotates, a coastal location moves through high tide and low tide zones.
  3. The Sun also affects tides.
  4. New Moon and Full Moon produce spring tides because the Sun, Earth, and Moon are aligned.
  5. Quarter moons produce neap tides because the Sun and Moon pull at right angles.

Note: "spring tide" means the water "springs" higher, not the season.
