Key Ideas

    Peripheral chemoreceptors respond strongly to low O₂, high CO₂, and low pH. Central chemoreceptors respond mainly to CO₂ indirectly through changes in H⁺ in cerebrospinal fluid. In this simplified model, the medulla adjusts autonomic cardiovascular output.
