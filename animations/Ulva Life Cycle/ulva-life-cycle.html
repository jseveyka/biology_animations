Note: Simplified as egg-like and sperm-like gametes; many Ulva species produce similar motile + and − gametes.

Ulva alternates between a diploid sporophyte and a haploid gametophyte. The sporophyte makes haploid zoospores by meiosis. Zoospores grow into haploid gametophytes. Gametophytes make gametes by mitosis. Fusion of compatible gametes forms a diploid zygote, which grows into the sporophyte.

They look similar, but they have different ploidy.
