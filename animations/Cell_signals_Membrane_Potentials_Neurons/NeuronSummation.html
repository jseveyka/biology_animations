Temporal and Spatial Summation

Fire excitatory synapses and watch EPSPs spread and add together at the axon hillock.

Summation at the axon hillock

Summation occurs when graded potentials overlap in space or time. The axon hillock receives the combined effect of many local postsynaptic potentials.
